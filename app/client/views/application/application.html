Why the application?

Hey there and welcome to the application process. Don't worry, it's not so much an application as more a registration form. However, we expect to receive more regirstations than we can accomodate, which is why - unfortunately - we will have to do some selection among the participants. We are looking to admit experienced hackers as well as first-time attendees to get a colorful set of attendees. This is why we ask you to fill out the motivation field if you're new to the whole hackathon business. This helps us to admit the people who really want to come, instead of someone who'll take up the space and eventually not show up.

For the more experienced hackers we've provided the fields for your CV and Github / website in order to get a glimpse at just how professional you are. Keep in mind that we don't want to intimidate anyone, so please, don't worry about not being good enough or not having a perfect CV.

We're looking forward to receive your application!

Basic Information
